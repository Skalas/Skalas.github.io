## Description

I've been making these for years and just perfecting and perfecting them 🤝🥞

I get asked about these DAILY - being honest I prefer these way more than regular pancakes I don't like regular pancakes at all BUT I'm not saying you have to have protein ones today have normal I just prefer these 😍

## Ingredientes:

* 40g Protein (cereal milk flavour is 10/10) (of course use any flavour but when I say it's a game changer)
* 40g Blended Oats (i use instant oats but you can just blend them)
* 1 Egg
* Half Tsp Baking Powder
* Half Tsp Baking Soda
* 40g 0% Greek yogurt
## Macros (without toppings):

* Calorías: 399
* Proteína: 45.5g
* Grasa: 10g
* Carbohidratos: 28.7g
## Macros (with toppings - 100g Greek yogurt):

* Proteína total: 58g 🤝
## Método:

1. Mezclar: Combinar todos los ingredientes en un bowl
1. Ajustar consistencia: The mix will seem dry just keep mixing - IF it's like powder some protein powders need more yogurt so if you need a little more then add some but you don't want runny mixture for thick pancakes
1. Calentar sartén: Hot pan to initially cook underneath them and then turn down to medium pan and let cook
1. Voltear: Flip them and then bring heat right down - take them off when they're 85% cooked as they continue to cook on the pan and you don't want them over cooked 🥞
## Sugerencias de toppings:

* Fruit
* Peanut butter
* Low calorie maple syrup from @myprotein
* 100g 0% Greek yogurt in the middle (brings protein to 58g)
## Tips Importantes:

### Consistencia de la mezcla:

* Debe verse seca al principio - seguir mezclando
* Si queda muy en polvo: Algunas proteínas necesitan más yogurt
* NO debe quedar líquida: Para pancakes gruesos necesitas mezcla espesa
### Técnica de cocción:

* Sartén caliente primero: Para dorar la parte de abajo
* Bajar a medio: Dejar cocinar a temperatura media
* Voltear y bajar calor: Después de voltear, reducir calor
* Retirar al 85%: Siguen cocinando en el sartén, no quieres overcooked
### Secreto del éxito:

"When you make them right you'll never have better pancakes - If you say they're dry you didn't make them right 🤝"
