## Description

Quickest protein dessert we've tried - under 5 minutes to make. Perfect lava cake consistency in the microwave.

## Ingredientes:

* ✅ 1 egg
* ✅ 75g fat free plain Greek yogurt
* ✅ 5g zero cal sweetener or sugar
* ✅ 18g cocoa powder
* ✅ 25g protein powder
* ✅ 10g semi sweet chocolate chips (melted)
## Método:

1. Mix: Combine all ingredients together in a microwave safe dish
1. Microwave: Cook for 60-90 seconds for lava cake consistency (or 3 minutes for brownie consistency)
1. Top: Add your favourite toppings
1. Enjoy: Serve immediately while warm
## Información Nutricional:

* Calorías: 260
* Proteína: 34g
* Carbohidratos: 17g
* Grasa: 8g
## Notas Importantes

### Por qué funciona:

* Greek yogurt adds moisture and protein without fat
* Protein powder bulks it up while keeping it macro-friendly
* Quick microwave method = perfect for cravings
* Melted chocolate chips create that lava center
### Tips de preparación:

* Use a microwave-safe ceramic dish for best results
* Don't overcook if you want the lava consistency
* Let it cool for 30 seconds before eating (super hot!)
* Adjust sweetener to taste
### Variaciones posibles:

* Add 3 mins for brownie texture instead of lava
* Use dark chocolate chips for richer flavor
* Top with protein ice cream
* Add berries or peanut butter
* Try different protein powder flavors (vanilla, cookies & cream)
### Perfecto para:

* Late night dessert cravings
* Post-workout treat
* Quick chocolate fix
* Meal prep desserts (make 2-3 at once)
* High protein diets
