### Chicken Pesto Bake

### Ingredients

* 250 g rice (any)
* 1 kg chicken breast, diced
* 100 g green pesto (Leggos brand in 5 g or aim for 120 calories per 47 g of pesto)
* 320 g low-fat Greek yoghurt
* 140 g chopped spinach
* 1 large red onion, finely diced
* 500 ml chicken stock
* 150 g sundried tomatoes
### Directions

1. Preheat oven to 180 °C / 360 °F.
1. Combine all ingredients in a large oven-safe bowl or baking dish (about 3 L capacity). Mix well.
1. Cover with alfoil for the first hour or so.
1. Bake for 75–90 minutes, until rice is tender and chicken is cooked through.
