## Description
A quick and easy 2-minute microwave protein brownie that avoids turning into a dry chocolate sponge. Perfect for satisfying sweet cravings during a calorie deficit, best served with some additional light cream or yogurt.

## Ingredientes
- 1 Whole Egg
- 100g (3.5oz) High Protein Greek Yogurt
- 20g (0.7oz) Cocoa Powder
- 20g (0.7oz) Sugar Free Maple Syrup
- 30g (1oz) Chocolate Whey/Casein Blend Protein

## Método
1. Add all ingredients into a microwave-safe bowl or ramekin and mix until smooth.
2. Microwave for around 90 seconds to 2 minutes (depending on your microwave).
3. Slightly undercook it if you want that gooey, fudgy brownie texture.

## Toppings (Optional)
- Vanilla yogurt
- Low-calorie ice cream
- Crushed nuts

## Notas
- **Texture Tip:** Slightly undercooking the brownie ensures a gooey, fudgy center instead of a dry, spongy texture.
- **Protein Type:** A Whey/Casein blend is highly recommended for microwave baking as it retains moisture significantly better than pure whey.
