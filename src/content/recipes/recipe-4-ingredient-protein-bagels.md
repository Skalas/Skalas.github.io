## Description

Perfect for meal prep, super easy, and 18g protein each. These are definitely the best bagel recipe - highly recommended using bread flour for fluffier texture!

## Ingredients (Makes 4 bagels)

* ✅ 250g bread flour (higher protein, better texture – or use all purpose)
* ✅ 20g baking powder
* ✅ Salt to taste
* ✅ 450g 0% Greek yoghurt
* ✅ Egg wash (1 egg + 1 tbsp water)
* ✅ Bagel Seasoning for garnish
## Method

1. 👉 Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and yoghurt until dough forms
1. 👉 Shape into 4 bagels
1. 👉 Brush with egg wash and sprinkle sesame seeds
1. 👉 Bake at 180°C (fan) / 200°C (no fan) for 25–30 mins until golden
## Nutrition (per bagel)

* Carbs: 44g
