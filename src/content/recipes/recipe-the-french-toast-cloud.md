## Description

Okay, these F*ck, you need to try! But make sure to add the caramel sauce and icing sugar!

## Ingredients

* ✅ 250g Vanilla YoPro
* ✅ 3 eggs
* ✅ 60ml egg whites
* ✅ 50g cornstarch
## Optional toppings:

* ✅ Icing sugar alternative
* ✅ Sugar-free caramel dulce de leche topping
## Method

1. Mix all ingredients together
1. Cook in the oven at 180°C for 50 minutes or until cooked through and golden brown on top!
## Nutrition (per serving)

* Protein: 25g
* Carbs: 27g
* Fat: 7g
