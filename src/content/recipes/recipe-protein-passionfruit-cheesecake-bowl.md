## Description
A simple, creamy, crunchy, and sweet protein-packed dessert made by turning vanilla protein yogurt into a thick vanilla labneh, layered with crushed sugar-free Maria biscuits and passionfruit pulp.

## Ingredients
- 160g (5.6oz) Vanilla Protein Yogurt
- 10ml (2 tsp) Lemon Juice
- 1 1/4 tsp Salt
- 30ml (1 fl oz) Passionfruit Pulp
- 4 Sugar-Free Maria Biscuits
- 20ml (0.7 fl oz) Maple Syrup (use sugar-free for lower calories if preferred)

## Método
1. Mix the yogurt, lemon juice, and salt together in a bowl.
2. Place into a cheesecloth, clean kitchen towel, or paper towel-lined strainer over a bowl.
3. Weigh it down gently with a bowl or mug and leave in the fridge for 24 hours until thick and creamy.
4. Press down lightly before unwrapping for extra thickness.
5. Layer with crushed biscuits and passionfruit pulp before serving.
